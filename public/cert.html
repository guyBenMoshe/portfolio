certifications & achievements

NX212 – Windows Forensics (City & Guilds Assured)

An international certificate from the City & Guilds of London Institute for completing the Windows Forensics track delivered by ThinkCyber

The course focused on identifying and investigating digital traces in Windows environments, including:

  • Analysis of file structures and NTFS artifacts (MFT, timestamps, ADS)
  • Detection of attack remnants and suspicious files.
  • Leveraging artifacts (Prefetch, Jump Lists, Amcache) to reconstruct attack timelines
  • Identifying and documenting initial compromise using advanced forensic techniques

The NX212 certification represents a globally recognized quality standard and demonstrates expertise in Windows forensic analysis

CERT–IL Advanced Cyber Course (CACC)

Certificate of completion for the CERT-IL Advanced Cyber Course, a 40-hour intensive program by Israel’s National Cyber Directorate. The course included hands-on exercises and lectures from industry experts, covering:

  • Incident investigation: NTFS artifacts, steganography, Windows Event Logs, and Network Forensics
  • Initial compromise detection and live investigation with evidence collection
  • Malware analysis: static and behavioral techniques, with focus on persistence mechanisms (Scheduled Tasks, Run Keys)
  • Attack timeline reconstruction and concise forensic reporting
  • Guest lectures on cyber excellence, unified defense platforms, and the role of AI in cybersecurity

This certificate highlights practical, real-world training in advanced digital forensics and cyber defense at the national level
